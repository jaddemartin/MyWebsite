I’m someone who finds joy in characters that are misunderstood and full of depth. My all-time favorite is Loki from the Marvel universe — if I could be anyone, I’d be a version of him. I connect with his chaotic charm, cleverness, and unpredictable heart.

I'm also a big fan of the Harry Potter world. I proudly stand with Slytherin — the house that values ambition and cunning. I find Draco Malfoy’s confidence both annoying and fascinating, and I really admire the Slytherin squad’s vibe. Though from another house, I also like Ginny Weasley for her fiery spirit.

In the galaxy far, far away, my favorite is Anakin Skywalker. Whether he's the tragic Jedi or the feared Darth Vader, he’s the Star Wars character I’m most drawn to.

I also collect Funko Pop figures — but not just any! I only buy characters I love, and unsurprisingly, most of them are different versions of Loki. After all, I’m just a variant of him, right?
